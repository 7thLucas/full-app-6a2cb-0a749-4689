# BLACKTOC — Creator-First Short-Form Video Platform

## Tagline
The creator-first short-form video platform paying 6x more than TikTok.

## What It Is
BLACKTOC is a full-stack creator economy platform and TikTok competitor. It is built for creators who want to own their audience, earn more, and grow faster. The platform pays creators $0.12 per 1,000 views — 6x more than TikTok's $0.02/1K — and offers 8+ monetization streams including subscriptions, tips, virtual gifts, pay-per-view, paid series, brand deals, affiliate links, and live stream gifting.

## Target Users
- **Creators**: Short-form video creators who want better pay, algorithm transparency, and anti-shadowban protections
- **Viewers**: Fans who want to support their favorite creators directly
- **Brands**: Brands looking for authentic creator partnerships
- **Admins**: Platform operators managing content, payouts, and users

## Brand Tone
- Empowering, bold, creator-first
- Direct and transparent (no hidden algorithm games)
- Premium but accessible
- Anti-corporate, pro-creator

## Core Value Props
1. 6x higher creator pay ($0.12/1K vs TikTok's $0.02/1K)
2. Algorithm transparency dashboard (creators see exactly what affects their reach)
3. Anti-shadowban guarantee
4. 8+ monetization streams
5. Creator co-ownership / equity program
6. Creator Bill of Rights
7. Instant payouts (no 30-day hold)
8. AI tools built-in (caption writer, thumbnail generator, script writer, hook generator, content coach)

## Strategic Principles
- Creator income is visible, trackable, and real-time
- No dark patterns — transparent algorithm, transparent earnings
- Mobile-first, swipe-driven video experience
- Gamification drives creator consistency and viewer engagement
- Growth tools (TikTok migration funnel, viral share cards, referral engine) accelerate adoption

## Anti-References
