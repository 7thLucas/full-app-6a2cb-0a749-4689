# BLACKTOC — Product Overview

## Product Identity
- **Name**: BLACKTOC
- **Type**: Creator economy short-form video platform
- **Tagline**: "The platform that pays creators what they're worth"
- **Core differentiator**: $0.12 per 1,000 views — 6× TikTok's $0.02/1K rate
- **Positioning**: Direct TikTok competitor built around creator-first economics and radical transparency

## Audience
- **Primary**: Content creators seeking fair compensation, transparent algorithms, and full content ownership
- **Secondary**: Viewers discovering, supporting, and engaging with creators through subscriptions, gifts, and watch parties
- **Tertiary**: Brands seeking authentic creator partnerships via the brand deal marketplace

## Strategic Principles
1. **Creator-first economics** — every product decision is tested against "does this make creators more money?"
2. **Radical transparency** — creators see exactly how the algorithm ranks their content
3. **Zero gatekeeping** — monetization from day 1, no minimum follower counts, no waiting periods
4. **Creator ownership** — creators own their content, their data, and their audience
5. **Instant liquidity** — no 30-day holds, no arbitrary payout minimums
6. **Creator equity** — co-ownership program gives creators a stake in the platform they build
